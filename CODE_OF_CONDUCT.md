# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

MeshFlow is governance infrastructure for production systems. We hold our community to the same standard.

We are committed to making participation in MeshFlow a harassment-free experience for everyone — regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, religion, or sexual identity.

## Standards

**Behavior that strengthens the community:**
- Giving and gracefully accepting direct, specific, constructive feedback
- Taking responsibility for mistakes and learning from them
- Focusing on what is best for the project and the people who depend on it in production
- Being welcoming to contributors at all experience levels

**Behavior that is not acceptable:**
- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form
- Sexualized language or imagery
- Personal attacks or political flamebait
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Gatekeeping or dismissing contributions based on anything other than merit

## Enforcement

Report violations to the maintainers at the email in `SECURITY.md`, or via a private message in Discord to a maintainer with the `@maintainer` role.

All reports will be handled confidentially. Maintainers who violate this code of conduct may be removed from their role by a vote of the remaining maintainers.

We follow the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) as the base for this document.
