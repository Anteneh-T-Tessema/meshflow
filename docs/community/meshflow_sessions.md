# MeshFlow Sessions — Annual Developer Conference

**Format:** Hybrid (in-person anchor + global livestream)
**Target date:** Q4 2026 (October–November window)
**Duration:** 1 full day
**Audience:** 200–400 in-person / 2,000–5,000 livestream

---

## Vision

MeshFlow Sessions is the one day per year where the practitioners building production agent systems share what actually works. No vendor pitches. No demos that only run in controlled environments. Talks selected entirely on "did this ship to production and what did you learn?"

The goal is to make MeshFlow Sessions the GOTO for the agent infrastructure community — the conference where the compliance-first, production-first developers feel at home.

---

## Positioning

- **Not another AI hype conference.** Zero Trust, audit chains, cost governance, crash recovery — the boring but critical stuff.
- **Real production stories only.** Every talk must include a production deployment. No prototype demos.
- **Regulated-industry focus.** A dedicated compliance track (healthcare, finance, energy, legal) that no other agent conference has.
- **Community-driven.** CFP open to everyone; talks selected by a community program committee, not sponsors.

## CFP (Call for Proposals)

### Timeline

| Date | Milestone |
|---|---|
| T-16 weeks | CFP opens |
| T-10 weeks | CFP closes |
| T-8 weeks | Program committee review complete |
| T-7 weeks | Speakers notified |
| T-6 weeks | Agenda published |
| T-2 weeks | Speaker slide review (optional, encouraged) |
| Event day | Sessions live |

### Selection criteria

1. **Production deployment required** — what are you running, at what scale, for how long?
2. **Specificity** — "we improved latency by 40%" beats "we improved performance"
3. **Transferable lesson** — what can someone take from your talk and apply next week?
4. **No pure product pitches** — company/product mentions are fine in context; a 25-minute ad is not

### Submission form fields

- Talk title
- 100-word abstract (public)
- 300-word detailed description (reviewer-only)
- Production deployment context (system type, scale, duration)
- What the audience will be able to do after this talk
- Speaker bio + social/GitHub
